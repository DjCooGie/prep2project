Ghanaian Naming Culture

Akan Names


The Ghanaian akan people name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.

These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of a person.


The form provided on the far right is intended to collect the day, month and year one was born and also the gender. On submitting, the form displays the respective Akan name of a person.
