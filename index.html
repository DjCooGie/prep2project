Ghanaian Akan Names


The ghanaian akan people name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.

These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of a person.

This webpage provides a form intended to collect the day, month and year one was born and gender. On submitting, the form provides the respective Akan name of a person.
